# MOTION_SPEC.md

# RishavendraOS Motion System Specification v1.0

## Purpose

Motion is the single most important component of RishavendraOS.

Without motion, the experience becomes a portfolio.

With motion, the experience becomes memorable.

The objective of the motion system is not to impress users.

The objective is to make users feel that the environment is alive.

Every movement should communicate:

- Intelligence
- Curiosity
- Discovery
- Connection
- Evolution

---

# Motion Philosophy

## Fundamental Rule

Motion must communicate meaning.

Never animate because something can move.

Animate because something has changed.

# Motion Hierarchy

Every motion belongs to one of five levels.

---

# Level 1 — Ambient Motion

Purpose:

The environment should never feel frozen.

---

Subtle environmental movement.

Floating architectural drift.

---

## Duration
