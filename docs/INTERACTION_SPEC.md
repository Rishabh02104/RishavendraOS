# INTERACTION_SPEC.md

# RishavendraOS Interaction Specification v1.0

## Purpose

This document defines every interaction inside RishavendraOS.

The goal is not usability alone.

The goal is emotional engagement.

Every hover, click, transition, zoom, and camera movement should feel intentional.

Users should feel they are exploring an intelligent system rather than navigating a website.

---

# Interaction Philosophy

Most websites:

Hover → Color Change

Click → Open Page

---

RishavendraOS:

Hover → Awareness

Click → Investigation

Explore → Discovery

Understand → Trust

---
