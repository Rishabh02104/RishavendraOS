# RishavendraOS Master Plan v2.0

## Vision

RishavendraOS is not a portfolio website.

It is a cognitive environment that visualizes how Rishavendra Sharma thinks, learns, experiments, and builds intelligent systems.

The objective is to create one of the most memorable personal websites on the internet.

Visitors should feel as if they are exploring a living intelligence system rather than navigating a traditional portfolio.

---

# Core Mission

Most portfolios answer:

"What has this person built?"

RishavendraOS answers:

"How does this person think?"

Projects are not the destination.

Projects are evidence.

The real product is the thinking process behind them.
