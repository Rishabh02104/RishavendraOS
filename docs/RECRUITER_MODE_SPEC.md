# RishavendraOS Recruiter Mode Specification v1.0

## Purpose

Recruiter Mode is not a page.

Recruiter Mode is a transformation.

Its purpose is to compress the entire cognitive environment into an instantly understandable professional profile.

Most recruiters spend:

30–90 seconds

evaluating a candidate.

RishavendraOS allows them to understand:

Skills

Projects

Technical Depth

Product Thinking

Career Direction

in under 30 seconds.

Recruiters often ask:

# Success Criteria

Recruiter can answer:

Who is this person?

What skills do they have?

What have they built?

Can they solve real problems?

Should I interview them?

within:

30 seconds

or less.

---

# Failure Criteria

Recruiter feels confused.

Recruiter must search for information.

Projects feel hidden.

Important information requires exploration.

---

# Ultimate Goal

Recruiter Mode succeeds when a recruiter says:

"I understand exactly who this person is and what they can do."

without needing to explore the full environment.
